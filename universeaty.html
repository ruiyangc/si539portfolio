I am Ray Chang, an aspiring UX designer & researcher, currently pursuing my Master's degree at UMSI.

Universeaty is problem-solving

Finding study spots is an important part of our life, but not necessarily to be time-consuming. The current ways for finding study spots are inconvenient and inefficient, so my intentions were clear when I framed the project: to help student to find their ideal study spots that meet their needs with minimum time and effort.

The Universeaty mobile application was a course project I researched and designed in 2019. When the instructor asked us what real-world problems we want to solve for this one-semester project, it took not much time for me to come up with this idea.
